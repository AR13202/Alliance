Current Transformers

Model Ref: CURRENT-TRANSFORMERS | Alliance Engineering Company

Alliance Engineering Current Transformers are precision-engineered for accurate current measurement, metering, and protection in LT and HT electrical systems. Featuring robust construction with high-grade materials, these units ensure long-term reliability even in harsh industrial environments.
