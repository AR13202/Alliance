Three Decades of Structural Excellence

Founded in Chandigarh in 1992, Alliance Engineering began with a singular vision: to bring world-class electrical engineering standards to regional infrastructure projects.

Through a commitment to rigorous testing and architectural clarity, we have expanded from a local consultancy to a national leader in high-voltage systems and industrial design.

"Our history is written in the stability of the grids we build and the trust of the partners we serve."
