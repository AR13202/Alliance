2.4 Core Specialisations Section 
Section Heading (H2) 
What We Manufacture 
Intro Copy 
From precision metering current transformers for HVPN and Powergrid substations, to rugged 
control transformers powering CNC and automation panels, Alliance Engineering delivers 
engineered reliability at every voltage level. 
Card 1: Current Transformers 
High-accuracy measuring and protection CTs built to IS 2705. Available in wound, bar-primary, 
and ring-core configurations. Accuracy classes 0.1 to 3, burden 2.5 to 30 VA. Suitable for 
energy metering, SCADA, and relay protection. 
Card 2: Control Transformers 
Single-phase and three-phase control transformers for machine tools, CNC panels, and 
industrial automation. Custom VA ratings from 50 VA to 10 KVA. Copper winding, laminated 
CRGO core, conformal coated for harsh environments. 
Card 3: Battery Chargers 
Float-cum-boost (FCBC) industrial battery chargers for substations, UPS systems, and telecom 
applications. Input: 230V AC ±10%. Output: 12V–220V DC, up to 200A. Compliant with IS 1869. 
Card 4: Potential Transformers 
Metering and protection class PTs as per IS 3156. Voltage ratios to 33 kV. Suitable for power 
metering panels, energy audit systems, and protection relays.

RECOMMENDATIONS 
6.1 Critical Content Fixes (Do First) 
• FOOTER: Replace 'Global HQ — 1200 Industrial Park, Silicon Valley, CA' with actual 
Panchkula/Chandigarh address on ALL pages. 
• FOOTER: Remove 'European Hub — Berlin, DE' unless you have an actual European 
office — fictitious addresses harm local SEO trust. 
• CONTACT PAGE: Update phone from +91 172 555 0192 (placeholder) to your real 
number. 
• CONTACT PAGE: Update email from contact@alliance.eng to your real domain email. 
• ABOUT PAGE: Replace placeholder staff names (Rajesh Khanna, Anjali Sharma etc.) 
with real team or remove the section entirely. 
• HOME PAGE: Change 'Industry Leader Since 1994' to '1992' to match your actual 
establishment year. 
• ALL PAGES: Change dropdown option 'Architectural Design' in contact form — not 
relevant to an electrical manufacturer. 
 
6.2 Schema Markup (JSON-LD) — Add to Every Page 
Add the following schema types in <script type='application/ld+json'> tags: 
• LocalBusiness / ManufacturingBusiness — Name, address, phone, geo, openingHours, 
logo 
• Organization — Name, URL, logo, sameAs links (IndiaMART, GeM, LinkedIn, JustDial) 
• Product — For each product page: name, description, manufacturer, brand, offers 
• BreadcrumbList — On all inner pages for better SERP appearance 
• FAQPage — On product pages with common buyer questions 
 
6.3 Products Page Recommendations 
Each product (CT, Control Transformer, Battery Charger, PT) should have its own dedicated 
page at /products/current-transformers, /products/control-transformers etc. with: 
• Unique H1 with primary keyword (e.g. 'Current Transformer Manufacturer in India — 
Alliance Engineering') 
• Technical specifications table (ratio, burden, accuracy class, IS standard, dimensions) 
• Downloadable datasheet PDF (also great for backlinks) 
• Application list: metering, protection, energy audit, SCADA, relay 
• Product schema markup 
• FAQ section targeting informational keywords

6.6 Page Speed & Technical SEO 
• Compress all hero images — use WebP format at max 150 KB for hero, 50 KB for 
product thumbnails 
• Add alt text to every image with keyword-rich descriptions 
• Ensure mobile responsiveness — especially the contact form and nav menu 
• Add XML sitemap at /sitemap.xml and submit to Google Search Console 
• Add robots.txt allowing all product, about, contact, and client pages 
• Install SSL (HTTPS) — required for ranking and trust 
• Target Core Web Vitals: LCP < 2.5s, CLS < 0.1, FID < 100ms
